How to Play
  • You and your opponent each play 1 card per turn (15 turns total).
  • Cards require a position — play cards that match where the match is happening.
  • Cards cost Stamina. Stamina trickles back each turn — manage it wisely.
  • Build Chains: play a setup card followed by its payoff card for combo bonuses.
  • Control improves your submission odds. Earn it through pressure, lose it to counters.
  • Win by submission finish or by leading on points when time expires.
  • XP from wins unlocks skills and belt ranks on the Athlete/Skills tabs.
