## How to play Performance 2

1. Tap **Performance 2**
2. See two separate groups on the board
3. **Try sparking first** (any rune) — watch a small cascade
4. Place your **one Fire** in the gap between the groups `(row 3, column 4 area — between them at row 3 col 4... actually gap is row 2 col 3)`
5. Spark again — full board lights up

Gap cell: **middle-right of left cluster** — empty cell at row 2, column 3 (between the horizontal line and vertical column).
