# GLYPH — Craftsmanship

**Status:** PRODUCT PRIORITY (Executive Producer) — **supersedes mechanics/content/progression work**  
**Authority:** Subordinate to [Studio Constitution](./STUDIO-CONSTITUTION.md) only  
**Mechanics:** Frozen · **No new systems · No progression · No content expansion**

**The Vertical Slice wins because it feels incredible, not because it contains more features.**

---

## The question (every decision)

> *If this mechanic never changed again… would this interaction still make players smile?*

If no — polish until yes.  
If yes — move to the next feel gap.

---

## What Craftsmanship is

| Prior priority | New priority |
|---|---|
| New gameplay systems | **Feel of existing loop** |
| Content expansion | **Delight in one board** |
| Progression layers | **Polish Performance 1 experience** |
| Strategic clarity | **Smile on every touch** |
| Feature count | **Constitutional feel proof** |

**Progressive Discovery** remains the learning philosophy — but **layer invitations are paused** until craftsmanship milestones prove the existing experience is delightful.

**Constitution unchanged.** Mechanics frozen. Hope Contract unchanged.

## Priority order (milestones)

Each milestone = playable build + build notes + targeted review questions ([Milestone Review Protocol](./MILESTONE-REVIEW-PROTOCOL.md)).

### C1 — Interaction Feel *(first)*

Picking up runes · dragging · dropping · selecting · sparking.

Every interaction intentional. One-thumb viable. No facilitator needed.

**Review question:** *Did placing and sparking feel satisfying without thinking about controls?*

### C2 — Cascade Feel

Travel · rhythm · timing · hope · curtain call.

The frontier feels alive. Eye follows the wave. Score stays hidden during performance.

**Review question:** *Did you watch the frontier instead of waiting for credits?*

### C3 — Visual Language

One coherent language: shapes · spacing · glow · typography · hierarchy.

One board. Improved lit/cold legibility. Calm identity.

**Review question:** *Did the board read clearly without explanation?*

### C4 — Audio

Interaction sounds · frontier sounds · curtain sounds · silence · comfort.

Audio confirms touch; cascade audio serves hope; silence is intentional.

**Review question:** *Would you play with sound on?*

### C5 — Mobile Experience

One thumb · comfortable · readable · fast.

Everything effortless on a phone. Safe areas · touch targets · no mis-taps.

**Review question:** *Could you play a full performance one-handed on your phone?*

## Success criteria (Vertical Slice)

The slice is ready when a player can:

1. Pick up and place runes **without friction** (C1 + C5)
2. Spark and **watch the frontier** with involuntary hope (C2)
3. Read lit vs cold **without squinting** (C3)
4. Feel the performance **with or without sound** (C4)
5. Voluntarily replay **because it felt good**, not because they need to learn (all)
