# GLYPH — Vertical Slice Feeling Map

**Status:** Production deliverable (CEO Amendment 3)  
**Authority:** Subordinate to [Studio Constitution](./STUDIO-CONSTITUTION.md) and the emotional contract.

**Purpose:** Every production milestone must support this timeline. When evaluating a feature, ask: *Does this increase the player's feeling that minute?*

**The Vertical Slice proves the Hope Contract — not GLYPH the product.** (CEO Amendment 1)

---

## The Hope Contract (unchanged)

> The player directs a performance they authored. They hope the frontier reaches one more rune. The game never lies about whether it did. Pride lands before the numbers.

---

## Minute-by-minute feeling map

### Minute 0 — Curious

**Game beat:** App opens. First frame. No modal.

| What happens | What they should feel |
|---|---|
| Dusk slate stage, quiet room | *"This looks intentional."* |
| Board empty, hand visible | *"Something goes here."* |
| ≤3 lines of copy total | Not lectured — invited |
| No score pressure yet | Safe to look |

**Hope signal:** Low stakes. The room is calm enough to stay.

**Failure:** Tutorial wall, loud UI, immediate target anxiety, greybox dev energy.

**Milestones that serve this minute:** M3 (minimum calm identity), M6 (whisper UI), M7 (micro-onboarding).

---

### Minute 1 — Comfortable

**Game beat:** First placements. Learning by doing.

| What happens | What they should feel |
|---|---|
| Tap rune → tap cell | *"I can do this with one hand."* |
| Soft placement feedback | Meditative, not fiddly |
| No punishment for exploring | Permission to arrange |
| Right-click / pick-up works | Control without friction |

**Hope signal:** Agency without confusion. They are **directing**, not decoding rules.

**Failure:** Mis-taps, unclear hand/board relationship, facilitator needed.

**Milestones:** M2 (interaction polish), M7 (onboarding).

---

### Minute 2 — Confident

**Game beat:** Board arranged. They understand the loop. Spark is imminent.

| What happens | What they should feel |
|---|---|
| Placed runes show spark-ready state | *"I'm ready to call Action."* |
| They chose the layout | Ownership |
| They know what Spark does (from doing) | Anticipation, not fear |
| Frontier HUD present but quiet | Something will happen *there* |

**Hope signal:** *"I built this. Let's see if I'm right."*

**Failure:** Player still unsure what Spark does; randomness feels like the decision.

**Milestones:** M1 (loop clarity), M2, M6.

---

### Minute 3 — Hopeful

**Game beat:** Spark → cascade → frontier performance. **The Hope Contract lives here.**

| What happens | What they should feel |
|---|---|
| Action beat — honest ignition | Performance begins |
| Travel line moves cell to cell | Eye follows the **wave** |
| Chain HUD rises; score hidden | *"Come on… one more…"* |
| Lit vs cold tells the story | Trust — the board is evidence |
| Rhythm accelerates but stays trackable | Escalation within calm |

**Hope signal:** Unprompted hope language. Player watches frontier, not credits.

**Failure:** Numbers during cascade, fake beats, desynced animation, player asks "what's my score?"

**Downloaded GLYPH today — this minute is the proof.

**Milestones:** M1 (cascade performance), M4 (animation breath), M5 (audio minimum — travel + spark).

---

### Minute 4 — Proud

**Game beat:** Curtain call → credits reveal.

| What happens | What they should feel |
|---|---|
| Cascade completes — brief hold | Exhale |
| Pride lands **before** numbers | Felt it, then confirmed |
| Credits appear after performance | §9 satisfied |
| Board shows lit/cold evidence | *"I caused that."* |
| Target comparison honest | Win or short — no lie |

**Hope signal:** Payoff ≥ planning. Player describes **what happened**, not only the number.

**Failure:** Score competed with frontier mid-run; anticlimactic resolve; casino stinger.

**Milestones:** M1, M5 (curtain resolve), M6, M8.

---

### Minute 5 — "One more."

**Game beat:** Voluntary replay. No prompt. No unlock wall.

| What happens | What they should feel |
|---|---|
| Returns to placement — not a feature menu | Loop is complete, not gated |
| Clear Board / New Hand available | *"I want to try again."* |
| No meta cliffhanger | Nothing locked behind tomorrow |
| Calm persists | Comfort destination holds |

**Hope signal:** **Voluntary second Spark within 10 minutes** without facilitator nudge.

**Failure:** One-and-done "neat"; player leaves; needs explanation of progression systems.

**Milestones:** M2 (replay affordance), M8 (integration), VS-2 gate metric.

## Greenlight Review (CEO Amendment 2)

At Vertical Slice completion, a formal **Greenlight Review** determines whether the Hope Contract earned the next investment.

| Outcome | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **GREEN** | Hope Contract proven in production build. Continue production. |
| **YELLOW** | Partial proof — revise and continue under evidence discipline. |
| **RED** | Hope Contract not proven. Stop the project. |

The Vertical Slice exists to **earn the next investment of time** — not to ship GLYPH.

---

## Production rule (before every feature)

1. Which constitutional principle does this strengthen?
2. PRODUCT or WORLD?
3. If removed, would the player's emotional experience become worse **at which minute**?
4. Is there player evidence supporting this work?

If answers are weak — **postpone**.
